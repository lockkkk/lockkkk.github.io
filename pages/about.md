Hi, I’m Jiaxin Lin. I’m an incoming assistant professor in the ECE Department at Cornell University, starting in Fall 2025.

I am currently a CS PhD student at the University of Texas at Austin. I am part of the [University of Texas Networked Systems (UTNS)](https://utns.cs.utexas.edu/) Lab. I am broadly interested in **computer networks**, **systems** and **architecture**, especially in programmable network devices, cloud computing, and data center networks. The overarching goal of my research is to design new software, hardware, communication protocols, and system abstractions that efficiently utilize the semantics and capabilities provided by advanced network hardware and interconnects.
